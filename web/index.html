How this maps to the production design

  • For a quick demo, the sender signs the future payout XDR and the recipient submits it later.
  • A production version should use a dedicated emergency account with multisig thresholds, rotating pre-authorized transactions, and clear expiry rules.
  • USDC support requires the recipient to establish a trustline before the emergency payout is created.
